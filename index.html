Overview

Infinite Shells is developed by Nocturnal Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play Infinite Shells on iOS.

We want to be completely transparent: Nocturnal Studios will never personally collect, store, sell, or use your data for any purpose — business or personal. We have no backend servers, no databases, and no access to who you are as a player.

Any data handling that occurs is done entirely by Apple (Game Center) and Google (AdMob) under their own privacy policies, which we do not control. We are simply the game developer — we never see or touch your data.

Information We Collect

Nocturnal Studios collects absolutely nothing from you directly. We have no servers, no accounts, and no data pipeline of any kind.

The following data may be collected automatically by third-party services embedded in the game — not by us:

  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings (Google AdMob)
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) subject to your device's tracking preferences (Google AdMob)
  • Game Center alias and leaderboard scores (Apple Game Center)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics (Apple)
We never collect your name, email address, location, payment info, or any personally identifiable information — ever, under any circumstances.

Advertising & Tracking

Infinite Shells displays ads via Google AdMob. On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission before allowing personalized advertising through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may decline tracking at any time and still use the full game — non-personalized ads will be shown instead.

You can change your tracking preferences at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

How We Use Data

Nocturnal Studios does not use your data for any purpose — personal or business. We simply do not have access to it.

Data collected by third-party services (Google AdMob, Apple) is used by those services solely to:

  • Display advertisements to support free gameplay (Google AdMob)
  • Maintain global leaderboard rankings (Apple Game Center)
  • Deliver crash reports and diagnostics to help us fix bugs (Apple)
Nocturnal Studios will never sell, rent, trade, or profit from your data in any way — now or in the future. This is a firm commitment, not a legal formality.

Children's Privacy

Infinite Shells is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our game, please contact us immediately at ranjel@me.com and we will take steps to delete that information.

Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any data retained by third-party services (Google AdMob, Apple Game Center) is subject to their own retention policies. You can request deletion of Game Center data through your Apple ID settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you by third-party services
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising at the device level
  • Withdraw consent for tracking via iOS Settings at any time
